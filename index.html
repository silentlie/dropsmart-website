Smart Water Meter

Control Your Flow, Cut Your Bills

DropSmart is a smart water meter that helps households monitor water usage, identify possible waste, and make smarter decisions before the next bill arrives.

The Problem

Water bills show the cost, not the cause.

Many households want to save water, but usage data, advice, and next steps are often hard to connect.

Unclear usage

Bills rarely explain which habits, leaks, or routines increased water use.

Scattered advice

Helpful guidance is spread across websites, council pages, and product recommendations.

Limited options

Renters and busy households need actions that do not require major changes.

The Solution

DropSmart turns confusion into a clear starting point.

DropSmart combines water usage monitoring with simple guidance, helping households understand patterns, spot possible issues, and choose realistic ways to save water.
