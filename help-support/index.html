Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need a plumber for installation? +

No — AquaWise is designed for simple DIY installation. Most setups take under 30 minutes using our step-by-step guide. However, if you have an older or non-standard meter, we recommend consulting a licensed plumber.

Can I use AquaWise if I am renting? +

Yes! Renters can use AquaWise in most cases. We also have a dedicated renters guide that outlines your rights, how to get landlord approval, and which government rebates you may still qualify for.

How accurate is the real-time monitoring? +

AquaWise sensors measure water flow with ±1% accuracy. Data is synced to the dashboard every 5 minutes, giving you near real-time visibility into your household consumption.

What if I see an incorrect reading? +

First, try refreshing your dashboard and verifying the sensor is firmly attached. If readings still appear off, submit a report via the contact form below and our team will investigate within 24 hours.

How do I apply for government assistance? +

Navigate to the Renter & Subsidy Info section in your dashboard for a full list of eligible rebates. We also have pre-filled application templates to make the process as quick as possible.

How do I reset my meter connection? +

Hold the reset button on the sensor for 5 seconds until the LED flashes blue. Then open the AquaWise app and tap "Add Device" to re-pair. The process usually takes under 2 minutes.
