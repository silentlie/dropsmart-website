About AquaWise

We believe every Australian household deserves simple and clear tools to manage their water usage — especially renters who face the most barriers.

The Problem

The Challenge

For most households, water bills are a surprise that arrives months too late. You can't manage what you can't see.

Renters face even steeper barriers: they often lack access to main meters, cannot install permanent hardware, and are disconnected from the data that dictates their costs.

Our Solution

The AquaWise Approach

We've built a non-invasive smart meter system that clips onto existing infrastructure without plumbing, providing real-time data to our mobile app.

By translating litres into dollars instantly, we give power back to households to adjust habits today, not after next quarter's bill arrives.
