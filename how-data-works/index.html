How Your Water Data Works

Your smart meter collects data and sends it securely to your water provider — you can view it anytime through their portal.

From Meter to Your Hands

Smart Meter Records

Your AquaWise meter records water flow data continuously throughout the day in real time.

Data Sent to Provider

Usage data is securely transmitted to your local water provider or council automatically.

You View It Online

Log in to your water provider portal to see your full usage history, bills, and patterns.

View Your Data Through Your Provider

We partner with leading utilities across Australia to ensure your smart data is integrated seamlessly into your existing account dashboard.

What You Can See

Unlock detailed insights about your household consumption.

Daily Usage

Track exactly how many litres your household consumes each day with hourly granularity.

Cost Estimates

Real-time estimations of your upcoming bill based on current usage trends and rates.

Usage Patterns

Identify peaks and troughs in your consumption to optimize laundry and garden watering times.

Leak Alerts

Get notified of continuous flow that might indicate a hidden leak or a tap left running.

Your Data is Secure

We employ military-grade AES-256 encryption for all data transmission. AquaWise is fully compliant with the Australian Privacy Act, ensuring your household usage patterns remain private and protected at all times.
