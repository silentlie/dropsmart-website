Renter Friendly

Renting? No Problem.

AquaWise requires zero modifications to your property. Our non-invasive ultrasonic sensors clamp onto pipes externally, meaning you don't need landlord permission or a licensed plumber. When you move, just unclip it and take it with you.

Common Installation Questions

What tools do I need for installation? +

No special tools are needed. The AquaWise sensor uses a simple clip mechanism that attaches to your pipe in seconds. Just your hands and the included instruction card.

Does it work with all pipe materials? +

AquaWise works with copper, PVC, PEX, and galvanized steel pipes. Our compatibility checker on the app will confirm your pipe type before you order.

Can it be installed outdoors? +

Yes — the AquaWise sensor is rated IP67 and can withstand outdoor Australian conditions including rain, dust, and temperatures up to 60°C.

What happens if my Wi-Fi goes down? +

The device stores up to 72 hours of data locally and syncs automatically once your Wi-Fi connection is restored. No data is lost.
